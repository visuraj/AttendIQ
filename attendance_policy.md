# Company Attendance Policy

## Work Hours
All employees must follow their designated shift timings. Standard hours are 09:00–18:00.
Operations staff follow 08:00–17:00. Sales team follows 10:00–19:00.

## Grace Period
A grace period of 10 minutes is allowed for late arrivals. Employees arriving within the grace
period are not penalized. Arriving more than 10 minutes late is flagged as "late".

## Late Arrivals
An employee is marked late if they arrive more than 10 minutes after scheduled start time.
Three or more late arrivals in a calendar month will result in a formal HR warning.
Repeated lateness may affect performance reviews and increment decisions.

## Early Departure
An employee leaving more than 15 minutes before their scheduled end time is flagged as early departure.
Early departures must be pre-approved by the team lead. Unapproved early departures are recorded
and may be treated as half-day leave.

## On Time
An employee is marked on time if they arrive within the grace period and leave at or after
their scheduled departure time.

## Absences
An employee who does not check in and has no approved leave is marked absent.
Three unplanned absences in a month will trigger an HR review.
Absences must be reported to the manager before 10:00 AM on the day of absence.

## Overtime
Leaving more than 30 minutes after scheduled departure is logged as overtime.
Overtime must be pre-approved and is compensated per company policy.

## Reporting
Attendance records are reviewed weekly by HR and department heads.
Monthly reports are shared with management by the 5th of the following month.
Employees can view their own attendance through the HR portal.
